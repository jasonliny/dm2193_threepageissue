Threat Modeling Basics

  1. What are your concerns?
  2. How would you rank these concerns?
  3. How much are you willing to compromise to address these concerns?

Example Situations

  • A whistleblower may want to protect themselves from a company or governemnt
  • The average American citizen may wish to keep their information private from large tech companies
